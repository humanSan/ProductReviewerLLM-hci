The Sangean RCR-5 Digital AM/FM Alarm Clock Radio is designed for convenience and flexibility with its dual alarm timer, offering weekday, weekend, daily, and once-timer selections. It features the HWS (Humane Wake System), allowing you to wake up gradually with a radio or buzzer. The clock also includes a snooze timer, adjustable nap timer, and adjustable sleep timer, making it easy to customize your sleep schedule. With digital tuning and 10 memory preset stations (5 FM, 5 AM), you can quickly access your favorite channels. The easy-to-read LCD display emits a soft, warm color that promotes a good sleeping environment, with an adjustable backlight to suit your preference. For more details, refer to the user manual in the technical specifications. The device also includes two alarm timers, a human wake buzzer, and an AC power adapter.
